Info about Business

Kat’s Custom Concepts is an art business designed to make custom pieces of art. These custom pieces could either be inspired by an already made creation (from the me or found concept) or I will work with you step by step to make you a custom piece of art based on the mediums I use. Currently I make hats, jewelry, potion bottles, 3-D prints, and other small items. You can see specifics and examples on specific pages for hats, jewelry, and other. If you have questions or would like to order, contact me via the information on my contact page.

Hi! I’m Kaitlin, I am currently getting a computer science degree, but in my spare time, I love to create art. My favorite kind of art is 3-Dimensional usable art, but I love trying it all! After having some friends buy my hats, I decided to start opening up some of my artwork to sell. When I was debating on selling my products, I thought back to what caused me to buy or not buy items. I realized that I often didn’t buy things because they never quite fit me. The necklace design would be pretty, but the stone wouldn’t be what I wanted. So, I decided to sell products that I could customize to each customer for a more personal experience and that you feel you are getting something perfect for you.
